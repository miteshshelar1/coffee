It is one of the oldest coffee shop located in the heart of the city which is not only a coffee shop but a heritage site too. The interiors are simple and vintage. Moreover they have an outdoor seatingarrangement where you can sip your coffee and munch your snacks under the green shade of trees. The place has the best filter coffee and South Indian Dosas and Upmas at a very economical price.
